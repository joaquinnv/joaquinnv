About Me

Backend engineer with a passion for scalable architecture

README.md

I'm a Senior Backend Engineer with 6+ years of professional experience and over a decade writing code. For the past 4+ years, I've been building the payment and event management infrastructure at Blackthorn.io, working across the full backend stack — NestJS microservices, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis — designing systems that handle thousands of daily transactions reliably.

What sets me apart is my ability to rapidly adapt to new technologies and bring innovative solutions to the table. From migrating 48 Salesforce classes to external storage to implementing purchase funnel analytics with Google Tag Manager — I don't just write code, I solve business problems.

Before Blackthorn, I gained versatile experience in Salesforce development, Azure cloud integrations (40+ enterprise integration flows), and full-stack development. I believe the best engineers are the ones who never stop learning — which is why I'm currently diving into LangChain, LangGraph, and AI Agents.
